It depends on the scope — ongoing operations roles, project-based builds, and one-off tasks are priced differently. Tell me what you need on a quick call or in an email and I'll give you a clear number up front. Hourly and monthly arrangements both work.
Can you work in my timezone?
Yes. I'm based in the Philippines and have spent years working with clients in the US, Canada, Germany, and the Middle East — including a year-long project across a 12-hour gap. I schedule overlap hours around your business day, and urgent items don't wait for "my morning."
Full-time or part-time?
Both. I've held long-term full-time-equivalent roles (1,800+ hours with a single client) and lighter ongoing arrangements of a few hours a week. We size the engagement to your workload, and it can grow as your business does.
How do we start?
A short discovery call or email exchange, then a paid trial week where I plug into your tools and clear real work. You judge the results before committing to anything ongoing. Most clients know within days whether it's a fit — my reviews suggest it usually is.
Which tools do you already know?
Shopify and WooCommerce for stores; Gorgias, email, and live chat for support; WordPress and CMS platforms for publishing; Slack and Google Workspace for teamwork; AppSheet, Google Apps Script, Supabase, Vercel, and GitHub for development and automation. New internal tools? I learn fast — clients have noted I adapt quickly to changing processes.

Of course. I've handled confidential customer data, internal databases, and private business processes for years — data confidentiality has been an explicit requirement in several of my contracts, and I treat it as standard practice.
